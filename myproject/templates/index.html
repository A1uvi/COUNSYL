What Counsyl Can Do

Journal

Using a speech-to-text API, COUNSYL prompts users to talk about their day and saves a summary of what they have said to their personal diary. This can be used as a reference when going to an actual therapist or as a tool to remember triggers.

Build Habits

Twilio allows COUNSYL to remind users to use the journal to encourage regular expression of feelings.

Why We Built This

Counsyl was created to use technology to help poeple who can't afford or locate mental health services. It is a free web app with many capabilities, ultimatey trying to help people feel confident in reaching out for proper medical support with their mental health issues.
